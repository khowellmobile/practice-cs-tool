Capybaras

Capybaras are the largest rodents in the world, native to South America. They are semi-aquatic mammals known for their friendly nature and social behavior.

  • They are excellent swimmers.
  • They live in groups.
  • Their diet consists mainly of aquatic plants.

The Mothman

The Mothman is a creature reportedly seen in Point Pleasant, West Virginia, in the late 1960s. It is often described as a large, winged humanoid.

  • It is associated with paranormal activity.
  • There are various theories about its origin.
  • The Mothman Prophecies book and movie popularized its legend.

Red Rising Book Series

The Red Rising series is a science fiction saga written by Pierce Brown. It follows the story of Darrow, a member of the lower class who rises up against a dystopian society.

  • The series explores themes of class struggle and revolution.
  • It features a richly imagined future universe.
  • The first book, Red Rising, was published in 2014.
